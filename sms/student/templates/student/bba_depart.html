About the BBA Department

The BBA Department focuses on developing innovative, ethical, and globally competent business leaders. Our curriculum is a perfect blend of academic rigor and industry exposure.

We provide students with hands-on training, real-world business projects, and mentorship from experienced faculty and business leaders.

Specializations Offered

Marketing Management
Financial Management
Human Resource Management
Operations & Supply Chain
International Business
Entrepreneurship

Our Vision

To produce globally recognized business leaders who drive innovation and ethical practices in the corporate world.

Our Mission

  • Deliver industry-relevant, high-quality education.
  • Promote leadership, entrepreneurship, and ethical values.
  • Encourage global collaboration and exposure.

Our Facilities

Business Simulation Labs

Experience real-time business scenarios using modern simulation tools for decision-making, market competitions, and financial planning.

Entrepreneurship Cell

Our EDC nurtures start-up culture with seed funding, mentorship, networking, and regular pitch events for aspiring entrepreneurs.

Industry Connect Centers

Collaborate with top companies on live projects and industrial training to develop real-world business solutions.

Smart Classrooms

Advanced smart classrooms equipped with interactive boards and technology-driven tools for immersive learning.

Digital Library

Access thousands of journals, business databases, and digital resources 24/7 to support advanced research and learning.
