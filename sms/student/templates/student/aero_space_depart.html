About Us

The Aerospace Engineering Department is committed to shaping future aeronautical innovators. We offer a blend of theoretical expertise and hands-on experiences in aerodynamics, propulsion, avionics, and space technology.

Our graduates contribute to global aerospace advancements, from next-generation aircraft to deep-space exploration vehicles.

Our Vision

To be a world-class center for aerospace education, research, and innovation.

Our Mission

  • Deliver industry-focused aerospace education.
  • Promote sustainable and space-grade technologies.
  • Collaborate globally to solve aerospace challenges.
